In-Situ Profiles

CTD profiles are collected by CCCFA and CFRF vessels while eMOLT profiles are collected by over 100 different fishing vessels throughout the Norteast. All profiles are processed by the CFRF team and made available via their ERDDAP server. Our dashboard uses the 1-meter binned CTD profiles (distinct salinity, temperature, and density values at 1-meter intervals), but complete, continuous CTD profiles are also available on the ERDDAP page. The eMOLT profiles can be downloaded here.
